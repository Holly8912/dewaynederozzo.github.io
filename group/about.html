Founded in May of 2014 and based in New York, New York, FanDestiny was created for the community of comic book lovers around the world. This is a platform for all to share fan fiction, self created fan art, and to commune with comic book lovers of like mindedness. We encourage creativity and respect for the creative arts and graphics, no more the genre. When we created FanDestiny we created with you in mind.
